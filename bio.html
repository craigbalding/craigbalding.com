Craig Balding - Biography

Craig Balding is an independent cyber security professional. He enjoys designing and creating practical solutions to a wide range of cybersecurity challenges facing organisations today and tomorrow. Prior to starting his own business, he was Managing Director for Cyber technology at Barclays PLC. He led all aspects of cyber architecture, engineering, assurance, innovation and Red Team. He joined Barclays in April 2014 as Managing Director for Group Cyber Security Risk. His responsibilities included understanding the threat landscape, defining the cyber risk control framework, setting risk appetite, owning policy and minimum standards and assessing global threat management capability.

Before Barclays, he worked at GE for 17 years, most recently as Red Team Director where he led a team of offensive security specialists to deliver a company-wide threat simulation service.

Mr Balding has 20 years experience in the IT industry.
