Welcome to Resilient!

Resilient Security provides cyber risk and security advisory, consulting and services that enable organisations to safely operate and transact online in the face of dynamic and adaptive threats.

Founded by Craig Balding, Resilient Security brings 20 years experience attacking and defending organisations, significant cyber insight and deep skills. He has considerable experience securing front and backend systems, including systemically important core platforms run by some of the worlds most valuable organisation in banking, industrials, manufacturing and healthcare.
